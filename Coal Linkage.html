Formulas
1. Quarterly Quantity

Q1 (April to June) = ACQ × 25%

Q2 (July to Sept) = ACQ × 22%

Q3 (Oct to Dec) = ACQ × 25%

Q4 (Jan to March) = ACQ × 28%

2. Monthly Quantity

Monthly Quantity = Quarterly Quantity ÷ 3

3. Daily Quantity

Daily Quantity = Monthly Quantity ÷ Days in Month

4. Selected Period Quantity

Total = Sum of Daily Quantities × Days in Selected Period

Coal Linkage Formulas
Quarterly Coal Distribution

Q1: (Annual Coal Quantity ÷ 4) × Q1%

Q2: (Annual Coal Quantity ÷ 4) × Q2%

Q3: (Annual Coal Quantity ÷ 4) × Q3%

Q4: (Annual Coal Quantity ÷ 4) × Q4%

Monthly Distribution

Each Quarter: Quarterly Amount ÷ 3 months

Rakes Calculation

Total Rakes: Annual Coal Quantity ÷ Quantity per Rake

Quarterly Rakes: Quarterly Amount ÷ Quantity per Rake

Monthly Rakes: Monthly Amount ÷ Quantity per Rake

Date Range Calculation

Financial Year: April 1st to March 31st

Q3: Oct-Dec, Q4: Jan-Mar
