Formulas

Energy Charges:
$$ = \frac{(\text{Coal Cost} + \text{Railway Freight}) \times \text{SHR}}{\text{GCV} \times 1000}$$
GCV:
$$ = \frac{(\text{Coal Cost} + \text{Railway Freight}) \times \text{SHR}}{\text{Energy Charges} \times 1000}$$
SHR:
$$ = \frac{\text{Energy Charges} \times \text{GCV} \times 1000}{\text{Coal Cost} + \text{Railway Freight}}$$
Railway Freight:
$$ = \frac{\text{Energy Charges} \times \text{GCV} \times 1000}{\text{SHR}} - \text{Coal Cost}$$
Coal Cost:
$$ = \frac{\text{Energy Charges} \times \text{GCV} \times 1000}{\text{SHR}} - \text{Railway Freight}$$
Specific Coal Consumption:
$$ = \frac{\text{SHR}}{\text{GCV}}$$
Landed Cost:
$$ = \frac{\text{Coal Cost} + \text{Railway Freight}}{\text{GCV}}$$
